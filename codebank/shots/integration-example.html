Features Demonstrated

  • ✅ Tap to capture screenshots
  • ✅ Long-press to show gallery
  • ✅ IndexedDB storage (no server required)
  • ✅ Auto-cleanup after 30 days
  • ✅ Responsive design
  • ✅ Download and share functionality
  • ✅ Export to HTML gallery

🔒 Privacy First

All screenshots are stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. No data is sent to any servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.
